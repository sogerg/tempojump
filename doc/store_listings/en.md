## Full Description

TempoJump is the show jumping rider's prep tool: calculate strides, plan your course and track your progress, before every round.

**Instant stride calculator**
Enter the number of steps counted between two fences and instantly get the theoretical and suggested number of strides, based on your horse's size, the footing and the speed.

**Related distances calculator**
Calculate the exact distance to set between two elements of a combination (double, triple), vertical or oxer.

**Cadence metronome**
Find and memorize the right canter cadence with a sound and vibration metronome, adjustable to your target speed.

**Annotated course plan**
Take a photo of your course plan, draw your track, place numbered markers and compare several track options (different colors).

**Training log**
Record every round: how it felt, placing, areas to improve, video. Share your recap and videos with your trainer in one tap.

**Exercises & Checklist**
Related distances for ground poles and cavalletti, pre-show checklist, unit converter (metric/imperial).

**Multiple horses**
Manage several horses or ponies, each with its own wither height and its own calibrated stride length.

Available in English and many other languages.

Full access to TempoJump requires a Pro subscription (3-day free trial included, cancel anytime).
